The Greymori Legacy

Since its founding in 2022, Greymori has stood firm in quality and sophisticated elegance. Our master blenders select only the finest tobacco leaves from all over the archipelago, carefully processing each blend of selected spices to achieve the perfect balance and character.

The Greymori name represents a commitment to excellence that has been passed down through generations. Every aspect of our production process adheres to the highest standards, ensuring a consistent and premium experience with every cigarette.

We believe that true luxury lies in the details, which is why we pay meticulous attention to every element of our products, from the tobacco blend to the packaging design.
